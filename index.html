FAQ

How does the consultancy process work?
  1. Session Preparation
    If all went well with the discovery call - we figure out if we're the right fit and if we can help achieve your goal - we'll have an understanding of your desired outcome and will plan the next call accordingly.

  2. Jump On The Call
    Your sessions will be private and conducted over the internet. We'll discuss your business, website, and how to move forward.

  3. Post-Session Work
    We will send you a detailed summary of the call over email, along with our recommendations and homework. All this information and more will be available via your Client Portal.

  4. Repeat
    Unfortunately, you won't see results from one call. It takes consistent effort and push to reach where you want to be, and we'll be there by your side every step of the way to help you through it.
